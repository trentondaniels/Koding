Once upon a time in Brittany, France...

Grandma Eliane had a dream for her children to go attend a university. To help make this dream a reality she would walk the streets of Brittany selling her baked goods to locals and tourists alike. With this money she was able to help my father pay for his schooling at Brigham Young University.

Then one day everything changed.

My father recieved a call that Grandma Eliane had passed away. When he came home a few weeks later he brought a small box with him. In it was every recipe my grandmother used to help raise the money for his tuition. Our family began making her food every Sunday to honor her legacy.

And they lived happily ever after.

Grandma Eliane's, my namesake's, recipes have become a cherished possession for my family. Her baked goods have become a source of comfort, pleasure, and nostalgia for us. Here at Eliane French Bakery we hope to share this hominess with you by providing the finest service, best prices, and of course authentic french baked goods made from Grandma Eliane's recipes.
