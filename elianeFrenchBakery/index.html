Fresh bread. Get it straight from the oven every morning!

Very few things are more french than a beautifully cooked baguette, and we have those in spades. We make our bread fresh every morning using a recipe straight out of the south of France. Come to us to get the finest bread in Utah county.

Flaky, buttery, airy French pastries, carefully crafted with the finest ingredients.

Who doesn't have a sweet tooth!? Here at Eliane's we beleive that indulging every once an a while (or every day) is not a crime. We have delicious French pastries, and even some pastries not native to France. To better satisfy our customers we even have gluten free options!

Your visit isn't complete without the perfect refreshing drink.

Unlike our competitors we understand that sometimes eating baked goods is thirsty work. That's why we offer homemade hot chocolate, freshly squeezed orange juice, and gourmet coffee, so that your experience with us will be perfect.
